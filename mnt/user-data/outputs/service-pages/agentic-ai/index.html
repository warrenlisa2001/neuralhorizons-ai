In Plain English

The Spectrum from Assisted AI
to Fully Autonomous

Most businesses are using AI at the lowest point of this spectrum. We build systems that operate at the top — autonomously, continuously, at scale.

Level 1 — Generative AI

Assisted AI

AI waits for a human prompt. Responds when asked. Produces content or answers. Still requires a human to act on every output. Valuable — but not transformative.

ChatGPT, Copilot, Claude chat
Level 2 — Agentic AI

Autonomous Execution

AI receives a goal and executes a multi-step plan to achieve it — independently. It uses tools, queries systems, makes decisions, and takes actions. No human required mid-process.

What we build — deployed now
Level 3 — AGI-Adjacent

General Intelligence

AI reasons across domains, sets its own sub-goals, learns from outcomes, and adapts strategy in real time. Narrow domain AGI is emerging in enterprise software today. We build with this horizon in mind.
